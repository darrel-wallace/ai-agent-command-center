Here is the research on the 2024-2025 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 Double Cab:

**1. Payload Capacity**
For a 4WD LT Double Cab, the maximum payload capacities are:
*   **2.7L TurboMax Engine:** 2,140 lbs
*   **5.3L V8 Engine:** 2,110 lbs

*Source: hendrickchevybuickgmcrichmond.com*

**2. Comfort Features**
On the LT trim:
*   **Heated Seats:** Available, typically as part of a convenience package.
*   **Wireless Android Auto:** Standard.
*   **Cruise Control:** Standard.

**3. LT Convenience Package II**
This package includes:
*   Bose Premium Sound System
*   Power sliding rear window
*   Hitch Guidance with Hitch View
*   In-vehicle Trailering App
*   Trailer brake controller

**4. Off-Road Packages**

*   **Z71 Off-Road Package:** This package includes:
    *   Z71 Off-Road Suspension with Rancho monotube shocks
    *   Automatic locking rear differential
    *   Skid plates (for engine oil pan, transfer case, and front differential)
    *   Heavy-duty air filter
    *   Hill Descent Control
    *   All-terrain tires
    *   A 2-speed Autotrac transfer case
    *   The payload for an LT with the Z71 package will be slightly lower than a standard LT due to the added weight of the off-road components.

*   **Trail Boss Trim:** The Trail Boss trim includes all the features of the Z71 Off-Road Package, plus:
    *   A 2-inch factory suspension lift
    *   Goodyear Wrangler DuraTrac mud-terrain tires
    *   The maximum payload for an LT Trail Boss is 2,117 lbs.

**5. MSRP**
The starting MSRP for the 2024 4WD Double Cab models are:
*   **LT:** $50,100
*   **Z71:** The Z71 is a package added to the LT trim; the price of the package is not listed as a separate trim level.
*   **LT Trail Boss:** $58,000

**6. Fuel Economy**
The EPA-estimated combined MPG for 4WD models are:
*   **2.7L TurboMax:** 19 MPG combined
*   **5.3L V8:** 17 MPG combined

*Source: daleearnhardtjrchevy.com*
