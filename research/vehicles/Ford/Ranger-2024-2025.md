#### Payload Analysis

**Factory Package Impact:**
- Base trim payload: 1,711 lbs
- Off-road package payload: ~1,671 lbs
- **Total payload reduction:** ~40 lbs

**Aftermarket Equivalent Analysis:**
Components user would likely add aftermarket:
- Skid plates: ~50 lbs
- Locking differential: ~20 lbs
- **Total aftermarket weight:** ~70 lbs

**Net Payload Impact:**
~40 lbs reduction - ~70 lbs aftermarket equivalent = **~+30 lbs net impact**

**Analysis:**
The factory FX4 package is a good value, offering integrated systems and warranty coverage for a minimal payload reduction compared to aftermarket additions.

## Strengths & Weaknesses Analysis

### Strengths
1. Excellent payload capacity in all 4WD trims.
2. Modern interior with standard wireless Android Auto.
3. FX4 package is a cost-effective and capable option.

### Weaknesses
1. Fuel economy with the 2.7L V6 is a step down.
2. Used market pricing may be on the higher end of the budget.
3. Comfort features like heated seats require package upgrades on XLT.

### Unique Considerations
- The Ranger offers a good balance of daily drivability and off-road capability without the extreme compromises of a Raptor.
