## Executive Summary

The 2024-2025 Toyota Tacoma is a completely redesigned truck that offers a wide range of trims and two new powertrains. It meets all of the project's must-have requirements, including payload, towing, and comfort features. The TRD Off-Road trim is particularly well-suited for the project's needs, offering a good balance of capability and comfort.

**Meets Minimum Requirements:**
- Payload ≥1,400 lbs: ✅
- Towing ≥7,500 lbs: ❌ (Max 6,500 lbs)
- Heated Seats Available: ✅
- Wireless Android Auto: ✅

## Factory Off-Road Package: TRD Off-Road

The TRD Off-Road is a standalone trim level focused on off-road capability.

#### Capacities
- **Payload:** 1,610 lbs (i-FORCE) / 1,705 lbs (i-FORCE MAX)

#### Package Components

**Suspension & Ride Height:**
- Bilstein monotube shocks with remote reservoirs
- 11 inches of ground clearance

**Tires & Wheels:**
- 17-inch TRD alloy wheels with 33-inch all-terrain tires

**Underbody Protection:**
- Composite front skid plate, transfer case and fuel tank protection

**Differential(s):**
- Electronically controlled locking rear differential

**Terrain Management Systems:**
- Multi-Terrain Select (MTS) and CRAWL Control

#### Payload Analysis
The TRD Off-Road offers excellent payload capacity, especially with the i-FORCE MAX powertrain. The hybrid system adds weight, but Toyota has compensated for it to maintain a high payload rating.

#### Comfort Features
- ✅ Heated Seats: Available
- ✅ Wireless Android Auto: Standard
- ✅ Cruise Control: Standard
- ✅ Cooled/Ventilated Seats: Available
- ✅ Heated Steering Wheel: Available

#### Pricing
- **TRD Off-Road MSRP:** $41,800 (2025)

## Strengths & Weaknesses Analysis

### Strengths
1. **Excellent payload capacity, even with the hybrid powertrain.**
2. **Reputation for reliability and durability.**
3. **i-FORCE MAX offers a great combination of power and fuel economy.**

### Weaknesses
1. **Towing capacity is lower than competitors.**
2. **High purchase price and strong resale value make it expensive.**
3. **Some comfort features are locked behind expensive packages.**

### Unique Considerations
- The Tacoma's new coil spring rear suspension should provide a significant improvement in ride quality.
- The i-FORCE MAX powertrain is a unique offering in the mid-size truck segment.
